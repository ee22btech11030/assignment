When a die is thrown, the probability of getting an odd number less than 3.\\
\solution
Let the random variable $X$ be defined as:
\begin{table}[!ht]
	\input{tables/table.tex}
\end{table}
\begin{align}
p_X(k)&=\begin{cases}
            \frac{1}{6} & \text{if }1 \leq k \leq 6\\
            0 & \text{otherwise}
        \end{cases}
\end{align}
Let $E$ be event to get odd number less than 3. since 1 is only odd number less than 3.
\begin{align}
\pr{E}&=p_X(1)\\
&=\frac{1}{6}
\end{align}
